WHAT IS CARDS FOR HUMANITY?

Cards for Humanity is a fast-paced online version of the popular card game, Cards Against Humanity, that gives you the opportunity to donate to children

in need - all while remaining as despicable and awkward as you naturally are.

Warning! This game contains offensive, profane, explicit content created by Cards Against Humanity and provided here, uncensored and complete, in digital form. Human Resources made us say that.

How To Play

Each player begins with

and will always have,

10 white answer cards

Step 1

For each round, one player

is randomly chosen

as the Card Czar

Step 2

Everyone else answers the black

question card by clicking on the

answer card they want to use

Step 3

The Card Czar then picks a

favorite answer, and whoever

that answer wins
